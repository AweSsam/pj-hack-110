In the Jungle, the Mighty Jungle

You take the lantern with you and decide to trek forward. After what seems like forever, you come across an old abandoned Bus Stop.

There doesn't seem to be any indication of a bus coming anytime soon and--- OHOHO, you see the glorious outline of a Vending Machine.

Inside the vending machine, you see your favorite hot cheetos on a HUGE DISCOUNT and you remember the coin in your pocket.

Do you use the coin and get yourself the cheetos (After all, you haven't had anything to eat yet), or do you save it?
