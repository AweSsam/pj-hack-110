In the Jungle, the Mighty Jungle

You arrive at a small stream of water. You drink as much as you can, without taking a break. You realize you need to take some water to go, otherwise you won't be able to survive the thirst.

Remembering the crinkle of foil in your pocket, you realize you have that tinfoil on you. You can either fashion a cup out of the tinfoil, or forage around for some other item that can hold water for you.
