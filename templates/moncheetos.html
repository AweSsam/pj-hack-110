In the Jungle, the Mighty Jungle

The monkey is still staring at you, but now he's more interested in those cheetos of yours.

You reach for your cheetos and hesitantly reach out to give him one. He snatches it from your hands, and you jump back.

He nibbles, and nibbles, before swallowing it whole... and then you realize something.

You realize that he bears a frightingely remarkable resemblance to Gordon Ramsey!

Even more scared now, you cower as the monkey sits there contemplating the cheeto... is it up to his standards? Why is he looking at you like that? Is he expecting something?
