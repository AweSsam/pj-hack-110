In the Jungle, the Mighty Jungle

Cutting down the thick folds of the jungle isn't easy, but you manage to do it with a stick you found next to you and your determination.

You're suddenly brought to a fork in the road. On the right you see a distant light which could potentially lead to your savior. On the left, lays a dark path, but you hear a stream of water coming from that direction. Which path do you choose?
